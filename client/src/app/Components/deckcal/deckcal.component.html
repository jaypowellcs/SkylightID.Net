Deck-Mounted Skylight Calculator

To use this calculator, measure the rough opening, as shown below.

A standard Velux Skylight will fit your rough opening. FS {{ deckMount?.sizeCode }} will be what you need to use.

Velux does not offer a standard skylight for this size, and they do not make custom deck mounted skylights. Your skylight might not be a deck mount or you will need to build a curb.
