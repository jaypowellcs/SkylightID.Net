Curb-Mounted Skylight Calculator

To use this calculator, measure the outside curb, including flashing of the skylight curb on the roof as shown below

A standard Velux Skylight will fit your outside curb dimension. FCM {{ curbMount?.sizeCode }} will be what you need to use. If your OCD is smaller then {{ curbMount?.outsideCurbWidth }}" and {{ curbMount?.outsideCurbHeight }}", you will need to furr out your curb to those dimensions.

Velux does not offer a standard skylight for this size, you will need to order a custom skylight.
